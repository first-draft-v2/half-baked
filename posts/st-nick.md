"They put chocolate in shoes?!
How eccentric, how odd!
And st. Nick on the 5th?
What a douche, what a fraud!"

And more words of the like
Were exclaimed in disgrace
As I wondered and sighed
"What is wrong with this place?"

No letters of chocolate
Or black slaves for me:
Cruel fate left me stranded
With crumpets and tea
