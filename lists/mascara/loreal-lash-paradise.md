Pros:
- Nice thickening effect
- Not too clumpy
- Easy to remove

Cons: 
- Leaves black specs on my upper lids while applying. Probably from my lashes hitting my skin before it dries. 
- Can smudge a little, esp. in hot weather
- The brush feels too full of product, so it’s prone to clumping. Also don’t like the generous dollop of product on the end of the wand when taking it out of the tube
- Doesn’t hold curl well
