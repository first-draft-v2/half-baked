method:
  - Whisk together the flour, baking powder, bicarbonate of soda and salt.
  - Mix the cooled coconut oil, tahini, water and vanilla until combined.
  - Mix in the sugar. Gradually fold in the dry ingredients until just combined, then fold through the chocolate.
  - Cover and chill for 10 minutes. Meanwhile, heat the oven to 180°C conventional and line two trays.
  - Scoop 3-tablespoon balls onto the trays, at least 7.5 cm apart.
  - Bake one tray at a time for 12 minutes, until the edges are set but the centres still gooey.
  - Leave on the tray for 20 minutes; the puffed cookies will settle and crack as they cool.
noteLabel: "Worth knowing:"
note: Weigh the ingredients, stir separated tahini thoroughly, and let the coconut oil cool. If the dough remains loose after chilling, give it a little longer.
