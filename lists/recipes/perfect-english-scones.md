    items:
      - name: Egg, beaten
        amount: 1
      - name: Water
        amount: 1
        unit: tbsp
      - name: Salt
        display: pinch
method:
  - Heat the oven to 200°C conventional. Line a baking tray.
  - Sift the flour and baking powder into a bowl, then stir in the sugar. Rub in the cold butter until the mixture resembles breadcrumbs.
  - Add the milk gradually, mixing with a fork, until you have a soft dough that is neither sticky nor dry. You may not need every drop.
  - Turn onto a lightly floured surface. Handle only until smooth, then pat or roll to 3 cm thick.
  - Cut into rounds with a 5–7 cm cutter. Put them on the tray and brush only the tops with the egg, water and salt mixture.
  - Bake for 15–20 minutes, until tall and golden. Serve warm with jam and clotted cream.
noteLabel: "Key to a good rise:"
note: Work the dough as little as possible and use conventional heat rather than fan.
